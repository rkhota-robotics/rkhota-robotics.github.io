About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur, where I lead research in robotic grasping and manipulation. My work involves exploring both model-based and data-driven approaches, including Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning, to develop advanced robotic systems.

My background includes a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Kharagpur, and postdoctoral research at Vrije Universiteit Brussel, where I focused on developing flexible and robust gripping solutions.

About Me

I am presently exploring model-based as well as Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning based methods to solve problems in robotic grasping and manipulation. My research focuses on the mechatronic design of multi-fingered robotic hands, grasp planning, and developing flexible gripping solutions for handling a variety of objects including deformable objects.

I have had the privilege of collaborating with leading robotics companies including KUKA Robotics and Atlas Copco, and have published extensively in top-tier journals and conferences.
