Welcome

I am Dr. Roshan Kumar Hota, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. My research focuses on robotic grasping and manipulation, employing model-based approaches alongside Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning techniques. With a background in mechanical engineering and extensive experience in robotics, I aim to advance the field through innovative designs and applications.

Supervisor: Prof. C.S. Kumar

Thesis title: Design Exploration of Underactuated Robotic Hands for Enveloping and Fingertip Grasps

  • Developed metrics for performance specification of underactuated hands in enveloping and fingertip grasps.
  • Performed robotic hand design optimization to obtain hand designs with superior grasping performance.
  • Designed and manufactured a five-fingered anthropomorphic underactuated hand actuated by a single motor.
